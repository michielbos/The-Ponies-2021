--- Money cheats ---
Rosebud - Adds $1,000 to household funds.
Motherlode - Adds $50,000 to household funds.
Filthyrich - Adds $1,000,000 to household funds.
AdjustFunds <amount> - Set household funds to the given amount.
AddFunds <amount> - Increase/decrease household funds with the given amount.
--- Buying/building cheats ---
MoveObjects on/off - Enables/disables moving of unmovable objects, selling of unsellable objects and placing objects inside each other.
--- Live mode cheats ---
MaxMuffins - Restore the needs of all ponies in the household.
--- Misc cheats ---
Expand - Expands the console.

Close - Closes the console.
ForceQuit - Quit the game.
